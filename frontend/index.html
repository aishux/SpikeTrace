Why SpikeTrace

SpikeTrace is your AI-native observability copilot. It connects to your logs, metrics, and traces to explain why that spike happened, which services are impacted, and what changed right before the incident. With natural language search, elastic-native analytics, and guided workflows, SpikeTrace helps SREs, developers, and analysts move from dashboards to answers in seconds.

1. Unified Telemetry

Ingest logs, metrics, traces, and alerts from your existing stack into a single elastic-native workspace.

2. AI Root-Cause Analysis

Let SpikeTrace correlate anomalies across services, deployments, and infrastructure to surface likely root causes.

3. Continuous Learning

SpikeTrace learns from every incident, alert, and query to refine patterns and recommendations over time.
