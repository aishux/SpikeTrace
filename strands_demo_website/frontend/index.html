Why SpikeTrace

Traditional tools tell you what is broken. SpikeTrace tells you the cost. By correlating system logs with financial telemetry and carbon metrics, we move SREs from dashboards to actionable business and environmental insights in seconds.

1. Unified Telemetry

Quantify wasted CO₂ emissions and excess compute runtime caused by recursive retries or inefficient deployment code.

2. AI Root-Cause Analysis

Instantly calculate revenue lost and total orders affected by service outages with our Agent which uses Elastic-native ES|QL correlation.

3. Continuous Feedback

Automatically generate Jira tickets and notify team on Slack channels with the correlated root cause and deployment ID.

The Triple Bottom Line Philosophy

At SpikeTrace, we believe every incident deserves instant clarity. Our mission is to bridge the gap between SRE reliability, Finance revenue, and ESG sustainability — all powered by Elastic-native AI.
